# Sales Outreach Campaign Results Report

## Overview
The recent sales outreach campaign targeted 20 leads, with a focus on understanding their interest levels and priorities. Below is a summary of the campaign's overall success rate, priority distribution, and insights into the personas that responded positively.

## Overall Success Rate
Out of the 20 leads contacted:
- **Total Leads**: 20
- **Interested Leads**: 12
- **Neutral Leads**: 1
- **Not Interested Leads**: 2

### Success Rate Calculation
The success rate is calculated based on the number of leads that expressed interest.

\[
\text{Success Rate} = \left( \frac{\text{Interested Leads}}{\text{Total Leads}} \right) \times 100 = \left( \frac{12}{20} \right) \times 100 = 60\%
\]

**Overall Success Rate**: **60%**

## Priority Distribution
The distribution of leads based on their priority levels is as follows:

| Priority Level | Count | Percentage |
|----------------|-------|------------|
| High           | 6     | 30%        |
| Medium         | 6     | 30%        |
| Low            | 8     | 40%        |

### Insights
- The majority of leads (40%) were categorized as Low priority, which may indicate a need for further qualification or nurturing.
- High and Medium priority leads each accounted for 30% of the total, suggesting a balanced approach in targeting leads with varying levels of urgency.
