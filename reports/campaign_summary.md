# Sales Outreach Campaign Results Report

## Overview
The recent sales outreach campaign targeted 20 leads, with a focus on understanding their interest levels and priorities. Below is a summary of the campaign's overall success rate, priority distribution, and insights on the personas that responded positively.

## Overall Success Rate
Out of the 20 leads contacted:
- **Total Leads**: 20
- **Interested Leads**: 12
- **Neutral Leads**: 2
- **Not Interested Leads**: 0

### Success Rate Calculation
- **Success Rate** = (Interested Leads / Total Leads) * 100
- **Success Rate** = (12 / 20) * 100 = **60%**

| Priority Level | Count | Percentage |
|----------------|-------|------------|
| High           | 6     | 30%        |

| Low            | 10    | 50%        |

### Insights
- **High Priority**: 30% of the leads were classified as high priority, indicating a strong interest from key personas.
- **Medium Priority**: 20% of leads showed moderate interest.
- **Low Priority**: The majority (50%) of leads were categorized as low priority, suggesting a need for further engagement strategies.
